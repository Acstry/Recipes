### Ingredients:
- 2 1/2 lbs pork shoulder
- 1 head garlic, minced
- 1/4 cup lemon juice
- 1/2 cup soy sauce
- 1/3 cup brown sugar
- 1/2 cup ketchup
- 1 can sprite or ginger ale

### Recipe:
1. Mix allow ingredients. Allow to marinate overnight.
2. Grill and serve.
