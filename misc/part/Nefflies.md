### Ingredients:
- 1 cup flour
- 2 eggs
- 1/4 cup milk
- salt (to taste)
- pepper (to taste)

### Recipe:
1. Fill medium saucepan with water. Set to boil. 
2. Mix all ingredients into a small bowl. 
3. Drop dough into boiling water in small bits. 
4. When dough floats, remove with a slotted spoon.
