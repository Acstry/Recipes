### Ingredients:
- egg - 1
- packed brown sugar - 0.25 cups
- pumpkin puree - 0.5 cups
- cornstarch - 1 tsp
- salt
- pumpkin pie spices
- evaporated milk - 0.25 cups

### Recipe:
1. whisk together egg and sugar
2. add in pumpkin puree, cornstarch, salt, spices, and evaporated milk
