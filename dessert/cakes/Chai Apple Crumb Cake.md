### Ingredients:
- [[Chai Streusel]]
- [[Butter Cake]]
- [[Chai Apples]]
- [[Glaze]] with Chai Apple Juice

### Recipe:
1. preheat oven to 350 degrees
2. grease or line a 9 in cake tin
3. once butter cake batter is in tin, top with apples and streusel
4. bake for 40-55 mins then let cool
5. top with glaze
