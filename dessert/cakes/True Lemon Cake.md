### Ingredients:
- 250g AP flour
- 2 tsp baking powder
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 150g salted butter, room temp
- 240g granulated sugar
- 3 eggs, room temp
- 45ml lemon juice & 25ml lime juice, mixed 
- Finely grated zest of 1 lemon and 1 lime
- 150ml whole milk
- addtl lemon/lime juice for brushing over cake
Icing:
- 150g icing sugar
- 30ml mixed lemon+lime juice

### Recipe:
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Butter and flour a 9x13 pan and set aside. 
2. Juice the citrus and stir juices together. 
3. Whisk together flour, baking powder, and salt. Set aside. 
4. In another bowl, add sugar and citrus zest. Rub together between fingers for 1-2 mins. 
5. With an electric mixer, beat butter and sugar until pale and fluffy. 
6. Turn mixer to low, add eggs one at a time, mixing well after each egg. 
7. Add citrus juice and mix to combine. Mixture may curdle, don’t worry. 
8. Add 1/2 of dry ingredients and mix just until incorporated. Add milk and stir to combine. Add remaining dry ingredients and mix again. Do not over-mix. 
9. Bake for 30-35 mins. 
10. Use reserved citrus juice to brush over hot cake, then set aside to cool for 15 mins. 
11. Mix together icing ingredients and pour over warm cake. Allow to cool fully before serving.
