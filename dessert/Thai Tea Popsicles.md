### Ingredients:
- coconut milk - 1 can (13.5 oz)
- coconut cream - 1 can (19 oz)
- sugar - 0.25 cups and 2 tbsps
- salt
- thai tea leaves - 2 tbsps
- vanilla extract - 1 tbsp

### Recipe:
1. heat all ingredients in a saucepan
2. strain mixture
3. pour into molds
4. chill for 2 hours then add sticks
5. chill overnight
