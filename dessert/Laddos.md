### Ingredients:
- almonds
- cashews
- desiccated coconut
- cardamom powder
- brown sugar
- melted butter
- rose petals/chopped pistachios

### Recipe:
1. roast almonds, cashews, and coconut for 5 minutes
2. blend with cardamom powder and 
3. bind with melted butter
4. rolls balls in rose petals or chopped pistachios
