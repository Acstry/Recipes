### Ingredients:
- premade croissants
- [[Chocolate Chip Cookies]] dough

### Recipe:
1. preheat oven to 350 degrees
2. cut the croissant in half length-wise
3. stuff cookie dough into croissant and put a dollop on top of croissant
4. bake for 8-10 mins
