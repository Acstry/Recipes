### Ingredients:
- flour - 3 cups
- salt - 1.5 tsps
- sugar - 1.5 tsps
- instant yeast - 1.5 tsps
- water - 1.5 cups
- olive oil

### Recipe:
1. mix dry ingredients then add water
2. let proof till doubled
3. shape dough into a ball and coat in olive oil
4. let proof in a buttered loaf pan
5. preheat oven to 375 degrees
6. bake for 45 mins
