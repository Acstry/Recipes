### Ingredients:
- oats - 2 cups
- bananas - 2
- nut butter - 1 tbsp
- grated carrot - 1
- vanilla extract - 1tsp
- baking powder - 1 tsp
- salt
- maple syrup - 1 tbsp
- milk - 1.25 cups
- chopped nuts - 1/3 cup

### Recipe:
1. mix ingredients together
2. bake at 360 degrees for 35-40 mins
