### Ingredients:
- 200g feta, sliced into thin blocks
- 50g AP flour
- 2 med egg, whisked
- 50g sesame seeds
- 100ml vegetable oil
- 2-3 tbsp honey

### Recipe:
1. Set out dredging bowls, one with flour, one with sesame seeds, and one with egg. Dredge feta in flour, egg, flour, egg, and sesame coating on all sides. 
2. In a frying pan over med high heat with oil. When hot, shallow fry feta on both sides until golden brown. 
3. Once fried, serve drizzled with honey.
