### Ingredients:
- 1 18oz jar castelvetrano olives, drained
- 1 tbsp garlic, minced
- 2 stalks green onions, chopped
- 2 tbsp gochugaru
- 1 tbsp maesil syrup (plum syrup)
- 1/2 tbsp apple cider vinegar
- 1 tbsp fish sauce
- 2 tbsp toasted sesame seeds
- 1 tbsp sweetener

### Recipe:
1. Mix all ingredients together in a large bowl. Serve.
