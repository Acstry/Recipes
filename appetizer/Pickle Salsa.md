### Ingredients:
- 1-2 tbsp olive oil
- 8-10 tomatillos
- 3 jalapeños, 4 if not using serrano
- 1 serrano (optional)
- 1 onion, quartered
- 3 cloves garlic, unpeeled
- 1/2 lime, juiced
- 1 bunch cilantro
- 1 32oz container pickles (she uses Grillo’s), drained but juice reserved
- 1/4 - 1/2 cup pickle juice, to taste
- tortilla chips, to serve

### Recipe:
1. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over med-high heat. Once hot, add the tomatillos, jalapeños, serrano, onion, and garlic. 
2. Char all for ~10 mins, flipping occasionally so all sides are are blistered and golden. 
3. Allow veggies to cool slightly, then peel garlic and transfer all to a blender. 
4. Add lime juice, cilantro, pickles, and pickle juice. Pulse to desired texture. Taste and adjust with lime and/or pickle juice. 
5. Serve with tortilla chips.
