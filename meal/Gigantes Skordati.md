### Ingredients:
- 85ml olive oil
- 2 shallots, finely chopped
- 1 tsp paprika
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- salt, to taste
- 2 cans butter beans, drained
- zest of half a lemon
- juice of 1 1/2 lemons
- 20g fresh parsley, chopped
- 20g fresh dill, chopped

### Recipe:
1. Heat oil in a pan over med-high heat. Add chopped shallots and saute for 2-3 mins, until softened. 
2. Add garlic and seasonings and cook until fragrant. Add butter beans, lemon zest, and lemon juice. 
3. Lower the heat and cover, allow beans to simmer for 10 mins. 
4. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Pour beans into large baking dish and bake for 20-25 mins, until golden. 
5. Stir through the herbs and bake addtl. 5 mins. 
6. Serve warm with pita or other crusty bread.
