### Ingredients:
- 1 kg chicken breast, cubed
- 500 grams yogurt
- 5 tbsp ghee
- onion
- handful coriander
- 5 chilies
- 2 cups of rice, cooked
- salt and pepper
- 1 tsp turmeric powder
- 1 tsp coriander powder
- 1 tsp chili powder
- 1 tsp cumin powder
- 1 tsp ginger garlic paste

### Recipe:
1. Sear chicken in ghee. Season with salt, pepper, seasonings, and ginger garlic paste.
2. Add onion, chilies, coriander, and lemon juice to the chicken.
3. In 2 tbsp hot hot ghee, add turmeric, coriander, and chili powder. 
4. Pour seasoned ghee onto properly whisked yogurt. 
5. Pour yogurt over chicken, mix, and serve over rice.
