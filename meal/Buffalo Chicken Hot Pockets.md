### Ingredients:
Dough
- 500g self-rising flour
- 520g greek yogurt
- 1 tbsp garlic salt
- 1 tbsp italian seasoning
Buffalo Chicken Filling
- 40 oz chicken breast
- 1/2 tbsp garlic salt
- 1/2 tbsp smoked paprika
- 2 tbsp ranch seasoning
- 1/2 tbsp pepper
- 1 cup white onion, diced
- 1/2 cup buffalo sauce
- 3 1/2 cups mozzarella
High Protein Buffalo Sauce
- 1 1/2 cups cottage cheese
- 2 oz cream cheese
- 1/2 tbsp garlic salt
- 1/2 tbsp pepper
- 1/2 tbsp onion powder
- 1/2 cup buffalo sauce
- 1/4 cup milk

### Recipe:
1. Combine all chicken filling ingredients, except mozzarella, together in a crock pot. Mix together and cook on high for 2-3 hours. 
2. Blend together all buffalo sauce ingredients. 
3. When chicken is finished, shred, top with buffalo sauce and mozzarella.
4. Knead dough ingredients together until smooth and split in to ten portions. 
5. On a floured surface, roll out portion until thin. Add on ~1/10th of chicken filling and a sprinkle of mozzarella. Pinch together edges and roll until filling is contained. 
6. Toast in a pan until golden brown. 
7. Can be folded into foil or airtight bags and stored in the freezer.
