### Ingredients:
Lasagna: 
- 1 pkg oven ready lasagna noodles
- 1/4 cup olive oil
- 1 container ricotta cheese
- 1/4 cup parsley, chopped
- 1 egg, beaten
- 1 pkg shredded mozzarella cheese
- 1 cup grana padano cheese, grated
- 1 cup parmesan, grated
Meat Sauce:
- 1 pkg lean ground beef
- Splash of dry red wine
- 1 can tomato sauce
- 1 clove garlic, chopped
- salt and pepper (to taste)

### Recipe:
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Add 2 tbsp olive oil to a large baking dish. Heat 2 tbsp oil and chopped garlic in a med sauce pan over med heat. Add in ground beef and tomato sauce. Add in sugar, salt and pepper, and a splash of wine. Turn the heat to low and let simmer for 1 hr. 
2. In a med bowl, mix together ricotta, egg, 1/4 cup grated parmesan, salt and pepper, and parsley. 
3. Cover the bottom of a large baking dish with a thin layer of meat sauce. Cover with 3 noodles. Top with 1/4 cup ricotta mixture, and 1/2 cup tomato sauce. Top with shredded mozzarella, parmesan, and grana padano. Repeat to make 2 more layers. For the last layer, add parsley on top. 
4. Bake covered for 35-40 mins. For the last few minutes, uncover. Remove from oven and let sit for 10 mins before serving.
