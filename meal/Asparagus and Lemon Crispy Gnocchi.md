### Ingredients:
- 1 pkg premade gnocchi
- Olive oil and butter 
- 1 banana shallot, minced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 small bunch asparagus, finely sliced
- Zest of 1/2 lemon
- Parmesan, to taste
- Basil, finely chopped. 

### Recipe:
1. Add gnocchi to a pan with olive oil and butter. Allow to brown and crisp. 
2. In another pan, gently fry shallot in addtl. oil and butter. 
3. With shallot, add in garlic, asparagus, lemon zest, and small squeeze lemon juice. Grate in parmesan. Allow to brown. 
4. Add in a splash of water, and salt and pepper to taste. Top with chopped basil. 
5. Allow sauce to thicken slightly. Add in gnocchi. 
6. Top with olive oil, more parmesan, black pepper, and red pepper flakes.
