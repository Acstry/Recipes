### Ingredients:
- 1.5lb bag little potatoes, diced
- Seasoning, to taste (looks like paprika, garlic and onion powder, oregano, salt, pepper, etc?)
- 1lb pkg smoked sausage, sliced
- 2 cans green beans
- 1 stick salted butter
- Jarlic

### Recipe:
1. In a crock pot, add in potatoes and season well. 
2. Add in sausage, green beans, and mix well. 
3. Top with one stick of butter and jarlic. 
4. Cover and cook on high for 4 hrs, or on low for six. 
5. Serve with noodles or bread.
