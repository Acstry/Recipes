### Ingredients:
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- 2 large onions, thinly sliced
- 6 garlic cloves, thinly sliced
- 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken thighs
- 1 1/2 cups red lentils, rinsed
- 1 tsp ground turmeric
- salt and pepper, to taste
- 1 cup mixed herbs (parsley, dill, mint)
- 1 cup plain yogurt
- 2 tbsp lemon juice

### Recipe:
1. Heat oil in a large pot and cook the onions until deeply caramelized, then add the garlic and soften it. 
2. Add the chicken, lentils, turmeric, salt, and plenty of pepper, tossing to coat.
3. Pour in 8 cups of water, bring to a boil, then simmer until the lentils break down and the chicken is cooked. 
4. Shred the chicken, return to the pot, and stir in the herbs, seasoning to taste. 
5. Mix the yogurt with lemon juice and a bit of salt. 
6. Serve soup topped with spoonfuls of yogurt.
