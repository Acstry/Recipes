### Ingredients:
- 1 lb lamb or beef, diced
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- 1/2 yellow onion, grated
- 2 tsp turmeric
- 1 tbsp ginger
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- 1 1/2 tsp pepper
- 2-3 tsp salt, to taste
- 1 cup tomato sauce
- 1/3 cup AP flour mixed with 1 cup water
- 1/4 cup cilantro, finely chopped
- 1/4 cup parsley, finely chopped
- 2/3 cup celery, chopped
- 1/2 cup chickpeas, drained and peeled
- 1/4 cup vermicelli noodles
- 2 eggs, beaten, optional
- lemon wedges, cilantro, and bread, to serve

### Recipe:
1. To a large pot over med high heat, add the olive oil, lamb, onion, and all spices. Brown meat for 2-3 mins until nicely browned. 
2. Add tomato sauce, 3 cups water, flour mixture, cilantro, parsley, and celery. Mix and bring to a boil. 
3. Drop heat to low, cover, and let simmer for 40 mins to an hour, or until meat is cooked and tender. 
4. Remove lid and add chickpeas and vermicelli. Mix and let simmer for about 12 mins or until vermicelli is cooked. 
5. Optionally, add beaten eggs and let cook undisturbed for 3 mins before stirring. 
6. If too thick, add more water. Taste and adjust seasoning. 
7. Serve with a squeeze of lemon, cilantro, and bread on side.
