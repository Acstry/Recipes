### Ingredients:
- 700g potatoes, peeled and cut into thin slices
- water
- salt
- 1/4 to 1/2 cabbage, cut into slices
- 1 onion, diced
- 400g ground beef
- oil
- 2 tbsp tomato paste
- 200ml heavy cream
- salt and pepper, to taste
- gouda or mozzarella cheese, greated

### Recipe:
1. Par-boil potatoes for a few mins in salty water.
2. In a hot pan with oil, fry onions until soft. Add in beef and brown. Add tomato paste, stir. 
3. Toss in cabbage, heavy cream, and seasoning. Allow to cook until cabbage softens and shrinks. Season to taste. 
4. In a casserole dish, add a layer of potatoes and season with salt and pepper. Top with beef/cabbage mixture. Top with cheese. Repeat lasagna-style until mixture is gone. 
5. Bake at 350 degrees until cheese is golden brown.
